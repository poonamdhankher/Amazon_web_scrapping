Verifizierter Kauf
I have used Old Spice shaving cream for over 40 years. My wife loves the smell. So do I. I was disappointed when the company that produced Old Spice stopped making the shaving soap bars for my Old Spice shaving mug. With this product, which comes in a tube, I just put a small amount in the shaving mug and use my brush to make the lather, as I did with the soap bars. It lathers well and "shaves" just like the shaving soap bars that came with the origingl Old Spice mug and brush. The tubes last quite a while, if you use a small dollop of shaving cream each time you shave. For travel, I leave the heavy mug at home and use a coffee mug or plastic cup where we stay to save weight. Some men just use a dollop and smear it on their face right out of the tube. I tried that. I wouldn't recommend it. Use a good quality shaving brush and lather up.

Rezension aus den Vereinigten Staaten vom 17. August 2021
Verifizierter Kauf
I recently got into wet shaving in an effort to slow down and be more caring to my ugly mug. Some of my fondest memories from early adolescence was my father coming out after a shave and filling the house with the intoxicating scent of Old Spice. Put simply, this new shave cream is not that. It has way too soft of a scent, and most of the smell is of powder. Basically you get smacked in the face with a sock full of powder. Now, that being said, some may love the scent. Its not bad, just not what I was hoping for.

The performance aside from the scent is rather nice though! Froths quickly(with a brush), feels nice on the face, provides plenty of slickness to allow the razor to glide over your skin, and not getting in the way of the razor slicing through all those pesky whiskers.

End of the day, its a good cream. Just dont hope for the old old spice scent when you squeeze the first dollop out of the tube.
